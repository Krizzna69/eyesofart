About the Artist

eyesof_art

Welcome to my artistic world. I am the creator behind eyesof_art, passionate about exploring visual expressions and capturing moments that speak to the soul.

My journey as an artist began when I started Drawing. I find inspiration in my husband eyes and specialize in him.

Each piece in my collection tells a unique story and represents a chapter in my artistic evolution. I hope my work resonates with you and sparks conversation or emotion.
